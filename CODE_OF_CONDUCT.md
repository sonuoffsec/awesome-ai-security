# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

AI Security Hub is a community for security researchers, students, red teamers, and defenders.

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors:**
- Sharing knowledge generously and accurately
- Being respectful of differing skill levels and viewpoints
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback professionally
- Crediting original researchers and sources
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behaviors:**
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing content intended for illegal use
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Submitting content designed to harm rather than educate

## Ethical Use Policy

All content in this repository is for **educational and authorized security research only.**

By contributing, you confirm that your content:
- Is intended for use in legal, ethical security testing and research
- Can be used in CTF competitions and controlled lab environments
- Is not intended to facilitate unauthorized access to systems

Contributions that exist solely to facilitate illegal activity will be removed.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the maintainers via GitHub Issues (use the `conduct` label) or by contacting the repository owner directly.

Maintainers will review reports and respond within 7 days. Enforcement actions range from content removal to permanent bans from the repository.
